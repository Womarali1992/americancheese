### METRONow

We’re enhancing essential services to ensure METRO is the first option our community chooses for its transportation needs. The multifaceted METRONow plan provides a roadmap for ridership growth, financial sustainability, and the establishment of key priorities that will strengthen the transit culture in the Houston region.

We're focusing on:

- Strengthening safety and security
- Improving cleanliness across the system
- Increasing reliability
- Expanding accessibility

# Houston

Before a project is built, a study must be done to determine if it will fill a transportation need, will not negatively impact the surrounding area and fits the department's overall plan. During the study phase, potential projects are analyzed to determine if they are cost-effective while also efficiently improving mobility. Some of these projects have moved beyond the study phase and are under construction.
